2 Days Scratch

Cosmic Combat (2024)

CosmicCombat is a 2D space shooter I made in Scratch for CS50 over a weekend. Instead of aliens, enemies are numbers—so kids are literally fighting math while playing. It features WASD movement, mouse aiming, two difficulty levels, and endless scoring that keeps going after you beat the main levels.

10 Days UE 4.27 (C++ & Blueprint)

Bouncy Pixel (2024)

I created Bouncy Pixel for Harvard's CS50 final project using Unreal Engine 4.27 with C++ and Blueprint. It's a Pong game supporting single-player and local two-player on one keyboard. The ball accelerates and leaves trails as your score increases, with dust particle effects on wall impacts for extra visual polish.
